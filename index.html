Who We Are

Founded in 1995, Meguerian & Associates is a trusted accounting and business services provider, offering its clients competitive rates and superior service. We prove to be the ideal destination for starting your business. Over the years, Meguerian & Associates has proudly helped countless businesses operate effortlessly and successfully.

You’ve already shown that you know what it takes to succeed; hard work, sacrifice, and dedication. This in turn has allowed you to start your own business. Meguerian & Associates respects your achievements and understands the time constraints placed upon you as a small business owner, especially when starting a new business. Our objective is to simplify the process by eliminating the middleman and saving you hundreds of dollars. Instead, we allow you valuable time to focus on operating and transitioning into your new business while Meguerian & Associates takes care of the rest.
